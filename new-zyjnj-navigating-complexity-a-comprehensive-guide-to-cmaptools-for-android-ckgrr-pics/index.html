Understanding Conceptual Maps and Their Power

Conceptual maps, often referred to as mind maps, are graphical representations of knowledge structures, emphasizing the relationships between concepts. They utilize nodes, representing ideas or concepts, connected by links, signifying the connections between them. These maps serve as visual frameworks for organizing, understanding, and recalling information. CmapTools for Android provides a user-friendly interface for creating these maps, facilitating knowledge construction and communication.
