A Legacy of Trust: The AA’s Role in Irish Navigation

The Automobile Association (AA) has been a cornerstone of road travel in Ireland for over a century. Its origins can be traced back to 1905, when a group of motoring enthusiasts in Dublin formed the first Irish Automobile Club. This organization evolved into the AA, establishing itself as a leading provider of roadside assistance, travel information, and mapping solutions.
